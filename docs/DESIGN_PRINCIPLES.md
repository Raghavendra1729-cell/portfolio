# Design Principles

## Core direction
- Keep the interface calm, minimal, and content-first.
- Prioritize clarity over decoration.
- Prefer subtle polish over visible novelty.

## Rules
1. **Hierarchy first**: clear headings, concise descriptions, and scannable cards.
2. **Consistent rhythm**: maintain spacing and padding consistency across sections.
3. **Limited motion**: only short, subtle transitions that support orientation.
4. **Low noise**: avoid bright color overload, dramatic effects, and ornamental UI.
5. **Action clarity**: CTAs should be direct and specific.
6. **Accessibility baseline**: semantic structure, focus states, and reduced cognitive load.

## Content style
- Use concise, high-signal language.
- Surface strongest evidence first (projects, execution, outcomes).
- Avoid filler copy and generic portfolio phrasing.
